Documenting historical performance reviews for managers and employees

Performance reviews are beneficial for both the manager and the employee. They give everyone an insight into the current performance of the employee and identify aspects where they can improve or upskill. Additionally, they provide and opportunity to discuss different ways of carrying out the current role, and are a platform to discuss the goals for the employee over the next period.
